Before fire, the world existed in endless grey, ruled by immortal dragons beneath stone arch trees.
When the First Flame appeared, Gwyn discovered a powerful Lord Soul and claimed its strength as his own.
Wielding devastating sunlight lightning, he led the gods in a war that brought the Age of Ancients to an end.
With the dragons defeated, Gwyn established the glorious kingdom of Anor Londo and began the Age of Fire.
For centuries he ruled as the Lord of Sunlight, but the First Flame eventually began to fade.
Fearing the rise of the Age of Dark and humanity's unknown future, Gwyn chose to sacrifice himself to the dying flame.
His sacrifice prolonged the Age of Fire, but it also created an endless cycle that doomed countless souls.
By the time you arrive in Lordran, Gwyn is no longer a godly king, but a hollowed husk waiting at the heart of the fading world.
