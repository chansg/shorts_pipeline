At the dawn of the world, one of its first lords became death itself.
Nito, the First of the Dead, found a Lord Soul in the flame, and it remade him.
The Death Soul fused his body into a towering mass of skeletons, bound by a miasma of decay.
When the gods warred on the Everlasting Dragons, it was his plague of death and disease that rotted them from within.
Victory won, he turned from the new age of light and descended into the deepest dark.
In the Tomb of the Giants he carved a light-devouring domain and lay down to slumber.
Yet even sleeping, he administers the death of all living things, quietly overseeing every end.
He is patient, for death always comes, and the First of the Dead waits still.
